Welcome to Meshtastic

A long range low power mesh networking system based off LoRa for exchanging messages, location data and much more. Start by connecting with your device.

Talk to your friends

In the chat tab you can start a new channel or join one by scanning the QR code. Multiple people can join the same channel. Chats are securedy by cryptography.

Share your position

On the maps tab you can share your position and see your friends pins around the map. Everything is made to work offline.

Fully configurable

On the settings tab you can configure notifications, range of your device, privacy options and much more.
